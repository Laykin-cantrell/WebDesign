Attack on Titan

A Manga Series by Hajime Isayama

SUMMARY

Attack on Titan is a manga series that stands out because of its intense storytelling, emotional characters, and dark atmosphere. The story follows humanity as they fight massive creatures called Titans while living behind three massive walls to protect them from the outside world. As the series progresses, it becomes increasingly complex as it explores themes of freedom, war, and morality.

Chapter 1: To You, 2,000 Years From Now

The first chapter introduces the world in the walls and shows Eren Yeager's frustration with living a restricted life, along with his friends Armin Arlert and Mikasa Ackerman. Their peaceful life was suddenly shattered when the Colossal Titan appeared and breached Wall Maria. Dozens of regular titans began invading the city, causing panic, death, and destruction. This chapter sets the tone of the series by showing how quickly safety can disappear.
