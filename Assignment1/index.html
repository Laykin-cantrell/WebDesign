Attack on Titan

A Manga Series by Hajime Isayama

Summary

Attack on Titan is a manga series that stands out because of its intense storytelling, emotional characters, and dark atmosphere. The story follows humanity as they fight massive creatures called Titans while living behind three massive walls to protect them from the outside world. As the series progresses, it becomes increasingly complex as it explores themes of freedom, war, and morality.

Chapter 1: To You, 2,000 Years From Now

The first chapter introduces the world in the walls and shows Eren Yeager's frustration with living a restricted life, along with his friends Armin Arlert and Mikasa Ackerman. Their peaceful life was suddenly shattered when the Colossal Titan appeared, kicking the Shiganshina District outer wall in and letting in all of the Titans. Not long after, another titan appeared, called the Armored Titan, and breached Wall Maria. Dozens of regular titans began invading the city, causing panic, death, and destruction. This chapter sets the tone of the series by showing how quickly safety can disappear.

Chapter 2: That Day

In the second chapter, the chaos continues as soldiers try to evacuate civilians and fight back against the titans. Eren and Mikasa race back to their house to find Eren's mother's legs trapped underneath a boulder. Trying to get her unstuck, a soldier named Hans comes by and tries to help the kids, but then a titan makes its way towards them. Eren's mother tells Hans her legs are broken and to leave her there and take the kids to safety. Hans listens and grabs the kids. As Eren is screaming and watching his mom, he sees her get grabbed by the titan and is eaten. This moment marks a major emotional turning point for Eren and deepens his hatred toward the Titans.
