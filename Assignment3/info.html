Hajime Isayama

Attack on Titan was created by Hajime Isayama. he wrote and illustrated the manga, which was fisrt published in 2009. Isayama became well known for his creating a story that combines action, mystery, and tension. His writing helped make Attack on Titan one of the most recognized manga series of all time.

WIT Studio

The first three seasons of the Attack on Titan anime were produced by WIT Studio. Their work helped bring the series to life with dramatic action, detailed backgrounds, and memorable animation sequences, especially in seaon three. WIT Studio played a major role in making the anime adaptation visually exciting and emtionally powerful for viewers.

MAPPA Studio

Later, the final season and movies of Attack on Titan was animated by MAPPA. The studio continued the adaptation and gave the later part of the story a darker and heavier visual tone unlike WIT Studio. With both WIT and MAPPA Studios contributing to the anime, Attack on Titan became known not only for its story but also for its strong animation and atmosphere.
